## For Android Devices

### Step 1: Download the APK
- Click the download link provided
- The APK file will download to your device (usually in the "Downloads" folder)

### Step 2: Enable Unknown Sources
1. Open **Settings** on your Android device
2. Go to **Security** (or **Privacy** on newer Android versions)
3. Find **Install unknown apps** or **Unknown sources**
4. Select your browser or file manager (Chrome, Files, etc.)
5. Toggle **Allow from this source** to ON

**Note:** On Android 8.0+, you need to allow the specific app (browser/file manager) to install APKs.

### Step 3: Install the APK
1. Open your **Files** or **Downloads** app
2. Find the downloaded APK file (usually named `app-*.apk`)
3. Tap on the APK file
4. Tap **Install** when prompted
5. Wait for installation to complete
6. Tap **Open** to launch the app

### Step 4: Launch the App
- Find the **Fruitzy** app icon in your app drawer
- Tap to open and start exploring!

## System Requirements

- **Android Version:** 6.0 (Marshmallow) or higher
- **Storage:** At least 50MB free space
- **Internet:** Required for Firebase and authentication features

## Troubleshooting

### "Install blocked" or "Can't install app"
- Make sure "Install from Unknown Sources" is enabled for your browser/file manager
- Try downloading the APK again
- Check if you have enough storage space

### "App not installed" error
- Uninstall any previous version of the app first
- Restart your device and try again
- Ensure your Android version is 6.0 or higher

### App crashes on launch
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure Firebase credentials are properly configured
- Try clearing app data: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Fruitzy ‚Üí Clear Data
